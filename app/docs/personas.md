# Personas

NextStep·AI is designed for three primary user types.

---

## 1. Priya — The Concerned Parent

**Age:** 36  
**Location:** Bengaluru, India  
**Board:** CBSE

**Background:** Priya is a working professional with one child in Class 6. She receives a report card every term but struggles to interpret what the grades mean in context. She wants to have a meaningful conversation with her child and teacher but doesn't know what to say.

**Goals:**
- Understand whether each subject grade is a real concern.
- Have a calm, productive conversation with her child tonight.
- Know what specific questions to raise at the parent-teacher meeting.
- Build habits at home that support her child without extra tutoring.

**Pain Points:**
- Report cards use jargon she doesn't understand ("satisfactory performance in cognitive domains").
- She doesn't want to overreact to a bad grade or underreact to a hidden problem.
- She has limited time — needs quick, actionable guidance.

**How NextStep·AI Helps:** She uploads the report card, gets a green/yellow/red summary in plain English, a conversation script for tonight, and a 30-day home plan she can actually follow.

---

## 2. Arun — The Class Teacher

**Age:** 29  
**Location:** Chennai, India  
**School:** State Board government school

**Background:** Arun teaches Maths and Science to two classes of 35 students each. He has limited time to give individual attention and wants to identify students who need support before they fall behind.

**Goals:**
- See which subjects are flagged red across his class.
- Identify patterns (e.g., most students struggling with fractions this term).
- Know which parents to proactively reach out to.

**Pain Points:**
- He can't personally track 70+ students' progress in detail.
- Report cards don't tell him about patterns — only individual grades.

**How NextStep·AI Helps:** The Class Patterns page aggregates flag distributions, highlighting where the class as a whole is struggling.

---

## 3. Meena — The School Admin

**Age:** 44  
**Location:** Hyderabad, India  
**Role:** Academic Coordinator

**Background:** Meena oversees curriculum and parent communication for a private school of 800 students. She needs visibility into teacher activity and parent engagement, and she manages the school's subscription plan.

**Goals:**
- Manage teacher and class assignments.
- Ensure parent accounts are active and engaged.
- Track subscription usage.

**Pain Points:**
- No single dashboard for academic and administrative oversight.
- Difficult to identify which parents are not engaging with report feedback.

**How NextStep·AI Helps:** The admin portal gives Meena control over classes, student rosters, teacher assignments, and the school subscription.

---

## Design Implication

All UI copy, error messages, and AI output tone are calibrated for **Priya** — a non-technical parent who is slightly anxious about her child's performance. Language should be warm, clear, and actionable — never clinical or alarming.
