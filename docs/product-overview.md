# Product Overview

NextStep.AI helps families and schools translate report cards into clear next actions.

The current product focuses on three connected audiences:

- Parents who need plain-language guidance after receiving a report card.
- Teachers who need visibility into class patterns and student context.
- School administrators who need tools to manage classes, students, teachers, and plans.

The core idea is simple: academic data should not stay trapped in confusing comments, marks, and disconnected observations. The app turns that information into structured guidance that can be understood and acted on.
