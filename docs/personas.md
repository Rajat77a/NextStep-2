# User Personas

## Parent

Parents use NextStep.AI to understand what a report card means and what to do next. They need clarity, reassurance, and practical steps rather than academic jargon.

## Teacher

Teachers use the app to understand class-level learning patterns and prepare more informed support. Their workflow should stay fast and focused.

## School Admin

Administrators manage the operational layer: students, classes, teachers, and subscription state. Their view should make repeated admin work predictable and easy to scan.
